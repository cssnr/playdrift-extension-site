Keyboard Shortcuts

These can be configured on your browser's extension management page and varies by browser.
Firefox: https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/manage-extension-shortcuts-firefox
Chrome: Google does not provide documentation on extension shortcuts. However, you can manage these by entering the following into your address bar: chrome://extensions/shortcuts
