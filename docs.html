Configure

You can access PlayDrift Extension through the Icon on the Extension Toolbar or Right Click Context Menu.

You can pin the Addon by locating the PlayDrift Extension icon on the Extension Toolbar, then;

  • Firefox, click the Settings Wheel and Pin to Toolbar.
  • Chrome, click the Pin icon.
